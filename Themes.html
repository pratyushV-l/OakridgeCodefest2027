How to choose

Pick a theme that gives your team a clear problem.

Strong projects usually solve one specific issue well instead of trying to do everything at once.

Pitch quality

Keep the idea demo-friendly.

The best entries are easy to explain in one minute and easy to show in a live demo.

Visual polish

Let the interface feel composed.

Formal spacing, crisp labels, and soft motion make the page feel more credible and complete.
