// OUR STORY

Innovation starts with curiosity.

Codefest was created to give students an opportunity to build technology that has real impact. Every edition introduces new challenges inspired by global problems and emerging technologies.

Students collaborate in teams, learn from mentors and present their solutions before experienced judges from academia and industry.

2017

First Oakridge Codefest launched.

2020

Expanded participation across schools.

2024

New AI and sustainability tracks introduced.

2026

Celebrating our 10th edition.

// OUR MISSION

Empowering students to solve problems that matter.

We believe the best way to learn technology is by building. Codefest encourages participants to explore bold ideas, experiment fearlessly and develop solutions that create meaningful change.
